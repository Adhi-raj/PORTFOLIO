ABACUS


learning abacus can provide numerous benefits to individuals of all ages. One of the most
notable benefits is the improvement of mental maths skills. The abacus requires users to
perform calculations mentally, thereby strengthening their ability to perform mental arithmetic.
Additionally, using an abacus can also enhance one's focus and concentration, as it requires complete
attention to complete calculations accurately. Beyond the cognitive and physical benefits, learning the
abacus can also instill a sense of confidence and achievement in individuals as they master this ancient yet powerful tool.
